What is Hire Purchase?

Hire purchase is a financing arrangement where you can acquire a vehicle by making regular payments over time. You get to use the vehicle immediately while paying for it in installments. Once all payments are completed, you become the full owner of the vehicle.

Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions

What areas do you serve?

We provide services across major cities in Nigeria including Lagos, Abuja, Port Harcourt, Kano, and Ibadan. We also offer intercity travel between these locations.

How far in advance should I book?

For regular services, we recommend booking at least 24 hours in advance. For airport transfers and special events, please book 48-72 hours ahead to ensure availability.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept cash payments, bank transfers, and mobile money transfers. Payment can be made before or after the service, depending on the arrangement.

Are your drivers licensed and insured?

Yes, all our drivers are properly licensed, insured, and undergo regular training. Our vehicles are also fully insured for passenger and cargo safety.

What is your cancellation policy?

Free cancellation up to 2 hours before the scheduled pickup time. Cancellations made within 2 hours may incur a small fee to cover driver costs.

Do you provide tracking for deliveries?

Yes, we provide real-time tracking for all our delivery services. You'll receive updates via SMS or WhatsApp throughout the delivery process.
